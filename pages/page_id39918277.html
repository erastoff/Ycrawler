About the role

We are looking for a Backend Engineer with strong Python and AWS skills.

At truemetrics, your tasks will include developing, improving, deploying and maintaining algorithms and data processing pipelines for sensor and geo data

As one of our first engineers, you get to be part of the rocket ship journey of a silicon valley funded tech startup. You will support us in scaling the team and there is a wealth of opportunities for you to grow with the company.

Requirements

  • You are living in Berlin or willing to move to Berlin within 3 months of starting with us.
  • You have your first professional experience as a backend software engineer.
  • You are an excellent Python developer with a focus on data processing and data science.
  • You have first experience in deploying to AWS cloud infrastructure (Lambda, S3, Athena, etc.).
  • You have a track record of developing algorithms for sensor and or geo data.
  • You can handle the dynamic environment of development in a startup: Ability to quickly write and deploy code at production quality without overengineering.

Bonus:

  • Experience in working with time-series ML models (data processing, training, tuning, etc.)
  • Do you have an impressive GitHub repo to prove your skills? Great!
  • Fluency in German gets you bonus points

What We Offer

  • Joining early as a key member of our development team with the opportunity of growing into a leading position.
  • Learn more and faster than ever before.
  • Prioritize, justify, and manage your daily tasks - no micromanagement.
  • Working partly remotely or fully onsite with us in our office in Kreuzberg (Berlin).
  • Sponsoring your move to the EU if applicable.
  • Competitive salary + equity in a fast-growing company

  1. First call with our CTO to get to know truemetrics

About truemetrics

The Way We Navigate Today Is Broken

I bet you know the following: "You Have Arrived At Your Destination!" â or so Google Maps thinks. In reality, the way couriers navigate is broken: No parking, hard-to-find entrances, and labyrinth-like apartment and office complexes can turn the last meters of delivery into a time-consuming puzzle. truemetrics provides parking positions and entrance locations to enable faster drop-offs.

Our data is generated by analyzing sensor data from drivers' smartphones, allowing truemetrics to identify precise parking and entrance locations, thereby simplifying the last meters of delivery.
